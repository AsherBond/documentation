Adding a Group

How to add a group.

To add a new group:

  1. Click All Accounts in the Quick Links section. The Accounts page displays.
  2. Click the ID of the account you want to add a group to. The account, name, and Registration status are highlighted.
  3. Click New groups in the Accounts page. The Create new groups popup displays.
  4. Enter the group name in the Group name field.
  5. Enter the group path in the Group path field.
  6. Click OK.
The group is associated with the account you chose. You can see the information if you select the account in the Accounts page and click the Member groups link, located in the Properties section of the screen.
