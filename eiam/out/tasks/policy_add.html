Adding a Policy

How to add a policy.

To add a policy:

  1. Click All Accounts in the Quick Links section. The Accounts page displays.
  2. Click the ID of the account you want to add a policy to. The account, name, and Registration status are highlighted.
  3. Click Add policy. The Add new policy popup displays.
  4. Enter the policy name in the Policy name field.
  5. Enter the policy content in the Policy content field.
  6. Click OK.
