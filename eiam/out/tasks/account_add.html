Adding an Account

How to add an account.

To add a new account:

  1. Click All Accounts in the Quick Links section. The Accounts page displays.
  2. Click New account in the Accounts page. The Create a new account popup displays.
  3. Enter an account name in the Account name field and click OK.
The new account displays in the list on the Accounts page.
