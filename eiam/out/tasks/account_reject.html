Rejecting an Account

How to reject an account.

To reject an account:

  1. Click All Accounts in the Quick Links section. The Accounts page displays.
  2. Click the ID of the account you want to delete. The account, name, and Registration status are highlighted.
  3. Click Reject. The Reject selected accounts popup displays.
  4. Verify that the displayed account is the one you want, and click OK.
The account's registration status displays as ??? on the Accounts page.
